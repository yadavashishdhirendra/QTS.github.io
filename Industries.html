Legal

Diamond Personnel specializes in the placement of legal professionals ranging from partner level attorneys to paralegals to legal proofreaders, and every position in between. We have been successfully providing legal staffing solutions to the country’s most prestigious law firms and corporate legal departments since 1996. We do this by continually providing the highest caliber employees at competitive rates. Our global database includes candidates ranging from former large firm partners and associates to recent graduates, and numerous seasoned professionals.

Temporary Legal Staffing Solutions

We maintain a vast database of skilled temporary legal personnel and are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ready to meet your temporary staffing needs as they arise. Our availability lists are updated daily to ensure that we are able to service our clients with the quickest and most efficient turnaround on job requests.

Permanent Legal Staffing Solutions

Diamond Personnel can help you find your next attorney partner, paralegal, proofreader, or other key staff members. We recruit our candidates from elite firms and leading universities to secure the best staffing choices. Our directory of candidates is continually updated to ensure the highest level of accuracy available.

Available legal staff includes:

  • Attorneys

  • Legal Proofreaders

  • Legal Word Processors

  • Paralegals

  • Legal Secretarial

  • Accounting and Billing Staff
